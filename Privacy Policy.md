# Effective Date: March 22, 2026

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how `MiniCalc` ("the Extension") handles user data. We are committed to ensuring your privacy is protected.

## 2. Data Collection
The `Extension` does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

We do not collect names, email addresses, or location data.

All calculations are performed locally on your device.

We do not track your browsing history or website data.

## 3. Data Storage
The `Extension` does not use remote servers. Any temporary calculation data exists only in the volatile memory of the `extension's` popup and is cleared when the popup is closed (unless you have specifically implemented local storage features).

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
Since we do not collect any data, we do not share, sell, or trade any information with third parties.

##5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions, please contact the developer via the support link on the Edge Add-ons Store.
